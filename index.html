Top Gun (1986)

"Top Gun" is a high-flying action drama that follows the story of Maverick, a talented but reckless fighter pilot, as he navigates the rigorous training program at the elite Top Gun Naval Fighter Weapons School. Alongside his equally skilled and competitive peers, including his loyal wingman Goose, Maverick aims to prove himself as the best of the best in aerial combat.

Top Gun: Maverick

"Top Gun: Maverick" is the direct sequel to the 1986 classic, picking up decades later with the iconic pilot, Pete "Maverick" Mitchell, now a seasoned flight instructor at the Top Gun Naval Fighter Weapons School. Despite his age and the changing landscape of aerial warfare, Maverick still embodies the same daring and Maverick spirit that made him a legend.

Iron Eagle

"Iron Eagle" is a high-octane action film that follows the daring exploits of Doug Masters, a young hotshot pilot determined to rescue his father, Air Force pilot Col. Ted Masters, who has been captured by enemy forces in the Middle East. With the assistance of his resourceful friend, Colonel Charles "Chappy" Sinclair, a retired pilot with a colorful past, Doug leads a group of misfit teenagers on a high-stakes journey to rescue his father before it's too late
